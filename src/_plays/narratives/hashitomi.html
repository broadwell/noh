--- title: Hashitomi ---

Introduction

Commenting on the general character of the play with attention to its intermedia particularity, how does it fit in the context of Noh repertoire and other '3rd category' plays.

Background

Time and Location
First Act: End of summer at dusk, at Unrin-in Temple in Murasakino, Kyoto. Second Act: At night, at the house of Lady Yūgao in Gojō, Kyoto.
Tsukurimono
Second Act: A house with a hinged lattice shutter
Characters
Mae-shite – Woman, ghost of a flower, MASK, COSTUME; Nochi-shite – Ghost of Lady Yūgao, MASK, COSTUME; Waki – Monk of Unrin-in Temple, COSTUME; Ai – A man living near Unrin-in Temple
Instruments
Kotsuzumi, ōtsuzumi & nohkan
Author
Attributed to NAITŌ Saemon
Translation
Rebecca Teele Ogamo

Plot

story in each of the acts
