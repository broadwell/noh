Dialogue → Monologue
Synopsis
Munechika, mindful of the difficulty of the task ahead, goes to pray for help to the family deity at the Inari Shrine.
Text
Prose.
Music
Unaccompanied vocal part of the waki delivered in kotoba.
Visuals
The waki stands up and walks to Square 1 to deliver the monologue facing the audience.

The delivers his monologue from Square 1, the most common space used by an actor for his first address to the audience. He confirms that he has accepted the Emperor’s request and that he intends to go pray at a shrine dedicated to his family deity: the fox (Inari no Myojin). In early Japan, the fox was the patron of swordsmiths and merchants.

During the last sentence, he performs a Left Step Pivot aiming for Square 6, which is about to represent the Inari Shrine.
