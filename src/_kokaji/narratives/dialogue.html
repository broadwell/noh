Plot
The Imperial envoy pressures Munechika to accept the imperial order.
Text
Dialogue between the waki and waki-sure with factual text set in kotoba.
Music
Unaccompanied dialogue.
Visuals
The Imperial envoy is kneeling at Square 5, Munechika at Square 9.

This dialogue is essentially an introduction to the coming waki’s monologue. Considering that this is the first out of three consecutive unaccompanied shōdan, the musicians and members of the jiutai put aside their instruments and fan, respectively.

The waki’s role in Kokaji is substantial, thus, it is the waki-tsure who is now and for the rest of the play sitting at the waki’s pole (Square 5).
