Form
First Act - Shite Exits:
Kakaru-2 → Ageuta-1
Synopsis
"The yūgao flower from Gojō" is the final hint about the identity of the apparition, who then slowly disappears into the shadow of the flowers.
Text
Poetry in regular rhythm.
Music
Set in hiranori, the chant of the jiutai in yowagin style is accompanied by percussion in strict rhythmic setting and a non-congruent nohkan part. This it the first entrance of the jiutai.
Visuals
The stage is opened up by the entrance of the jiutai. The action of the shite is limited to simple movements within Squares 1 and 2.

The first ageuta is the dramatic and intermedia climax not only of the Shite Exits dan, but also of the whole first act. It brings a new and refreshing sound: the jiutai, the last performer who had not yet taken part in the play. The jiutai’s first entrance always delineates an important moment in a play, hence it's special name – shodō. The ageuta is also the first shōdan accompanied by the full hayashi – the two percussion instruments and the nohkan. It produces thus far its richest and most consolidated texture. The hiranori chant and its percussion accompaniment in strict rhythm, brings the first use of poetic text in congruent setting.

The narrative introduces an important piece of information that helps indentify who could the woman be. In the very first line, it gives the location of a dwelling in Gōjo, which in the Genji monogatari is associated with Lady Yūgao’s home. Being the first line of an ageuta, it is given great emphasis by being delivered twice, separated by the uchikiri.

The final passage involves stage movement that is related to the text. While the jiutai sings that ““In the moment the eyes turn aside, she becomes a dream.” the shite takes Six Steps Forward, in preparation for a Right Circlet, which he then performs to mimic the woman’s disappearance, as announced by the jiutai: ““…as she disappears in the shadow of the flowers.””. As the melodic line, supported by the nohkan’s mi roku no ge pattern, moves from the high range to medium, preparing to cadence in the low range, the shite performs the standard cllosing pair of movements Forward Pointing and Open-retreat. The shite can now leave the stage, which will be done slowly and in silence.
