Visuals
The shite slowly traverses the bridge, as he moves toward the Mirror room, where in preparation for the second act, he will change kimono. Even, as he walks out off the stage, the shite stays in character. The slow and quiet exit is well suited for a spirit of a lady.

The shite’s exit can be sometimes accompanied by music. This is the case for instance, when the shite is an important character such as the deity in Kokaji. However, Hashitomi’s shite is a young lady so her elegant, slow (minute-and-a-half) exit is performed in silence.
