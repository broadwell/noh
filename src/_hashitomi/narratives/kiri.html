Plot
The conclusion of Genji’s poem. Lady Yūgao asks the priest for his prayers and disappears inside the house.
Text
a sung dialogue between the shite and the jiutai, set in ōnori
Music
Kotsuzumi & ōtsuzumi (strict), nohkan (non-congruent)
Stage-space
Dance-to-text occupying for the most part the three strongest stage positions: Squares 9, 4 and 3.
