Hagoromo – Kuri

The module is named after the pitch kuri from the higher register. For the most part, the pitch material of the piece focuses on the High pitch with few incursions on the Kuri shown with highlighted text on the video.

Upon reaching the kuri’s signature cadential melisma, the hon-yuri, the pitch material switches to the Medium before cadencing on the Low. (The pitch material in this performance is a major second lower than the one shown in the Figure above).
